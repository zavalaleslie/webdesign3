OUR MISSION

Quotez Art provides underserved youth with free, exceptional programs in academics and arts within a nurturing environment, empowering them to develop their potential, pursue their education and strengthen their communities.

INSPIRING & CHALLENGING

We build on students' sense of safety by engaging them in art, validating early accomplishments, nurturing their interests, and making connections to other topics they can explore.

ENGAGEMENT & NURTURING

Once students feel strong in themselves and in their relationship with our program, we begin to push them more. We challenge students to think bigger about their futures and their potential, and we inspire them with possibility.

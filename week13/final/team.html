    About Captain Bob

    Whether you are a die-hard fishing addict or a novice, Live Seamus Services offers a variety of charter services from Yacht Harbor in Los Angeles, California, to give you an exciting and memorable fishing experience. As the top sport fishing charter in the area, we guide clients so they can enjoy the pursuit of the multi-species of fish that are available fishing in the Delta.

    Since Bob could walk, he was already hooked on fishing with his father. Heck, it must had been in the Soo Hoo genes because Bob's son was also fishing before he could even walk! Fishing has different meanings for every angler and for Bob, it is to enjoy the elements of the great outdoors, the challenge, the excitement and unpredictable outcome of fishing. He has a U.S. Coast Guard 100 ton Masters license and trained in first aid and CPR.

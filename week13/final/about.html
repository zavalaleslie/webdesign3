    Reservations and Cancellations

    Reservations require a 50% deposit at the time of booking.
    A cancellation of 30 days or more prior to the booking date will result in 50% of the deposit being refunded.
    A cancellation within 30 days of the booking date will result in a loss of deposit.
    The full deposit may be applied, one time, to a future date in the same calendar year because we know, life happens and we will work with you to get you back out in the water.
